title=Adjuvant radiotherapy in Stage I endometrial cancer. Where do we stand?
from:Eur J Gynaecol Oncol
time=2003
abstract: This paper reviews the anatomical spread and failure patterns of surgical Stage I endometrial cancer. The controversial aspects of the optimal adjuvant treatment are presented. An attempt is made to identify the most effective management approach based on the pertinent literature data.
