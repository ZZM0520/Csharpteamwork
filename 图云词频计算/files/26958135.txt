title=Endometrial carcinoma located in the right septate uterus cavity: a case report.
from:Pan Afr Med J
time=2015 Nov 20
abstract: Endometrial cancer in patients with uterine congenital malformations is exceptional and there are only a few rare cases published in the literature. We report the case of a 67 years-old patient with an endometrial cancer located in the right cavity of a complete septate uterus.
